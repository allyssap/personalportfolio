Women In Chemistry Club Website

About

This was a project hosted by the UWindsor Google Student Developer Club. My team and I were tasked with designing and creating a website for the UWindsor Women in Chemistry Chapter.

I designed the navigation and footer on the site, as well as embedded the google calendar and twitter feed for upcoming events they want their club members to know about and to show the most recent tweets from their twitter account.

Goals and Accomplishments

With this project, my main goal was to help the Women in Chemistry Windsor Chapter to have a site to show off their information, their club and their events. I also wanted to work with a team on creating and coding a website in HTML and CSS. With this website, I also worked with the bootstrap framework

With our busy school schedules, this project took around 3 months to complete.
